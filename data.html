Qubole

Qubole provides a cloud-native data platform that simplifies big data analytics. With automation, scalability, and multi-engine support, Qubole enables data teams to focus on insights rather than infrastructure management.

  • Support for Spark, Hive, Presto, and Airflow
  • Unified data lake access across AWS, Azure, and GCP
