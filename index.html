About Us
Vatsin was founded in 2012

with the primary objective to offer services locally for Neverfail, a BCDR solution. Later, it was replicated for vendors that partnered with us following best practices.

Vatsin is a progressive ICT Management and Services Company with customers across Middle East, Africa, and India. Vatsin is focused on Sales Consulting, Channel Enablement, Building Pre-Sales Capacity, Marketing and Lead Generation, primarily working with Vendors, who have products and services that need focus in sales, these could be startup companies or companies who wants to accelerate revenue and profit. We work with them and enable them to achieve their goals."

In 2015, with the acquisition of Neverfail by Artisan Infrastructure, we started to offer XaaS (Anything-as-a-service). This created a new avenue for our business via opex model. Many customers opted for this model as it reduced the burden of upfront investment. We also tied up with Arcserve, which provides exceptional solutions to protect the priceless digital assets of organizations in need of full scale, comprehensive data protection. Arcserve also supports multi-platform like Windows, Linux and AIX.

In 2018 we started offering Digital Transformation solutions - helping enterprises build robust products with niche engineering services in DevOps, Cloud, Container, Full-Stack product, and Test engineering.

2020 was a milestone year for Vatsin, wherein we started offering Cloud Solution from MS and AWS, followed by Cybersecurity. We offered solutions in SAM (software Asset Management), Data and AI, which consolidated our Digital Transformation journey.

To complement our niche offerings, we signed up with Circularo – a digital signature Company offering Arabic support, Karanji – eLearning solutions for Corporates.

Our objective is to give the customer - comfort and the vendor - the support that helps in increased revenue. Our passion and commitment to what we do is the cornerstone to our value proposition. Our clients' success is our mission and inspiration. Satisfaction of our clients’ is our success.
